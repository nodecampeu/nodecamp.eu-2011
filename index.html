What?

An informal event for developers interested in node.js to socialize, learn, create, have fun, share, network... The event will be a product of your participation ... though we may cheat a bit and will have a handful of interesting speakers on hand. This is a two-day event with workshops, talks, fun and party! ;)

Where?

Coworking Cologne is collaborative working space located centrally in Cologne, Germany. It’s home to a number of freelancers in the region, numerous tech user groups, among them Cologne’s Javascript meetup, the region’s first FabLab DingFabrik and numerous other creative endeavors. It’s located centrally in region as well and is easily accessible from many European locations. Similiar events have successfully taken place here in the past such as last summer’s Railscamp.
